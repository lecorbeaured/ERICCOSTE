Common Questions

Do I need any prior knowledge about credit?

Not at all. These books are written for complete beginners. I explain everything from the ground up, assuming you're starting from zero.

How is this different from free information online?

Free content is scattered and often contradictory. These books give you a clear, organized path from start to finish, no jumping between tabs or wondering what to do next.

How quickly will I see results?

It depends on your situation, but many people see changes within 30-45 days of disputing items. The books give you realistic timelines and expectations.

What format are the books in?

All books are digital (PDF) so you can start reading immediately. You can read on any device: phone, tablet, or computer.
